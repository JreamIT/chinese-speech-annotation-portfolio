# Key Findings

## Fast Speech

Fast speech produced more phrase-level errors than normal speech.

Example:
历史 → 地址

## Background Noise

Background noise generally preserved sentence meaning but increased phrase distortion.

Example:
学习环境 → 水喜欢鸡

## Numbers

The model handled standard numbers well but struggled with informal monetary expressions.

Example:
二毛五 → 2.5

## Chinese-English Mix

Mixed-language speech was the most difficult condition.

Examples:
Python → 拍賞
due → 敢對
cheat sheet → 7-C
